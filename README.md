## AUTOMOTIVE SECTOR STOCK ANALYSIS

## OVERVIEW
this project aims at providing comparison among the stock prices of 5 major automotive companies over a span of
5 years from 2021 to 2026

## STOCKS ANALYZED
`TSLA` `HMC` `GM` `F` `TM`
automotive sector was chosen to compare performance across american and japanese manufactureers during a period 
of EV transition and supply chain disruption

## Analysis performed
-daily percentage changed
-Cumulative returns
-50 day simple moving average
-Rolling standard deviation
-volume analysis
-maximum drawdown
-best and worst performing stocks

## Key findings
from the overall analysis it can be concluded that `GM` has highest growth rate and `MC` shows the lowest growth rate
Highest rolling volatility: `TSLA` and  Lowest rolling volatility: `HMC`
Among the 5 stocks the worst case historical crashing point(maximum drawdown) was highest for `TSLA`
Highest average volume : `TSLA` (99M shares) ; Lowest average volume: `TM`
